Praful

Praful loves and respects his parents the most. He was born on the banks of the river Ganges in Allahabad, India. He is Hanumanji's bhakt (a devotee of lord Hanuman) since birth also (But cannot recite the Hanuman Chalisa as fast as Sonal). He also grew up climbing trees, but throughout 4 different countries. Through his adventures, he made a lot of close friends. They admire his humour, affection and blatant honesty. He is a vegetarian that loves eating Fake Meat and anything fried and cheesy. After graduating high school in Malaysia, he went to America to pursue his degree and got stuck their by his ambition to change the world. He is now on the Product team at Runnable.com in San Francisco.

Sonal

Sonal also loves and respects her parents the most. She was born in Lucknow, India. She is Hanumanji's bhakt since childhood and she can recite the Hanuman Chalisa in less than 2 minutes. She grew up climbing trees and running other sorts of ruckus in town. She has a lot of close friends who admire her vicacious and mischievious nature; as they also know that inside she is a soft and caring person. She doesn't take kindly to people trying to upsell her (especially Rikshaw walas, Dominos Delivery guys and street peddlers marking up the MSRP on soft drinks). She adores desi mangoes and foreign strawberries. She basically loves anything sweet. Her soft hearted sweetness does not mean she is not determined! Her hardwork, mental strength and determination has lead to her current position as Team Leader in JP Morgan Bangalore.

It is a coincidence that both their favortie movie is "Andaaz Apna Apna".

How we met

They were introduced by their parents. Initially things were slow, Praful wrote to Sonal and she wrote back. They arranged to meet on Skype. One Skype session and it was BOOM (well atleast according to Praful :)). They knew they were instantly attracted to each other. Both of them had a feeling of similarity in terms of charachter, morals and up bringing. It really seemed to them that this was not the first time they were meeting. After 8 months of extended Skype sessions (sometimes up to 20 hours a week!) and incessasant Whatsapp messages, it finally came time to meet each other person. On Februrary 25th 2014, they finally met. To their relief, they both acknowledged that reality was not so different when compared to Skype. Praful quickly decided to make a move and seal the deal fast.

The Proposal


Praful setup a nice arrangment with Taj Vivanta in Bangalore. He got Sonal's favorite strawberries, candles and deserts. He escorted Sonal to the setup, engagement ring in hand. As as Sonal sat down, Praful got on his knees and proposed.
It then came time to break the news and celebrate with the Family.

THE ENGAGEMENT

Praful and Sonal got engaged in Allahabad, the ancestral home of Praful. Close family memebers were invited from both sides of the family and there were immense celebrations.
